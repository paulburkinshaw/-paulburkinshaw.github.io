Paul has been developing software since he learned to program his ZX Spectrum at 8 years old. He's progressed to slightly more powerful machines since then but his love for software development remains.

Now in his 13th year in the industry Paul has developed solutions for desktop, web, mobile, and cloud platforms using a wide range of front and backend technology. He now focuses on full stack development across the C# /.Net / JavaScript stack.

He has consulted for startups, worked with small to large businesses in the public and private sector in the construction, utilities, and retail industry as well as clients in the charity and voluntary sector. Recent clients include BP, EDF, and Royal Mail.
